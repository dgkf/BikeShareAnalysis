About

This project was conducted as a capstone for The Data Incubator during the Summer of 2016.

Although conceptualized and submitted as part of an initial application to the program, the project matured largely under the leadership of the wonderful instructors and curriculum provided through The Data Incubator program. Their help and guidence in teaching the tools and providing feedback for the project was indispensible in its development.

Data was generously provided via public data accessible through The Bay Area Bike Share Open Data Initiative and the Center for Transit-Oriented Development
